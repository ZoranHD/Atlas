ABOUT US

Manufacturing garmet for some of the most respectable world fashion brands.



Our team consist of hightly skilled craftsmen. Some of them with more than 30 years of experience in various apparel production phases. We are dedicated to quality, precision and flexibility. These qualities qualified us for production of clothng for some of the world's most demanding fashion markets (France, Germany, Italy). Depending on your project requirements, we are able to provide cut/sew as well as full package services. We welcome established brands, as well as a new fashion startups. From small to large scale production - we give flexibility to the brands to turn their creative ideas into a finished product. Working with the Atlas means working with a reliable, professional and flexible clothing production partner in Serbia.
